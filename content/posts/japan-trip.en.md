## Overview

A trip to Japan covering Tokyo, Osaka, and Kyoto. Highlights include Tokyo Tower, Osaka Castle, Kinkaku-ji, and various anime-related spots.

## Tokyo

- **Tokyo Tower**: Stunning views from the observation deck
- **Shibuya**: The famous crossing and vibrant nightlife
- **Akihabara**: Anime and manga paradise

## Osaka

- **Osaka Castle**: Historic castle with beautiful grounds
- **Dotonbori**: Street food heaven - takoyaki, okonomiyaki
- **Kuromon Market**: Fresh seafood and local delicacies

## Kyoto

- **Kinkaku-ji** (Golden Pavilion): Breathtaking golden temple
- **Fushimi Inari**: Thousands of torii gates
- **Arashiyama**: Bamboo forest and scenic river views

## Tips

- Get a Japan Rail Pass for unlimited train travel
- Convenience stores (konbini) are your best friend
- Cash is still widely used - carry yen
- Try local ramen shops for the best experience
